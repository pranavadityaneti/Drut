3. Digital delivery — no shipping

Drut is a purely digital service. Nothing is physically shipped or delivered to you. When your payment succeeds, your Drut Pro access is granted immediately and electronically on your Drut account — you can use the paid features straight away on the web and in the apps where you are signed in. Because there is no physical product, no shipping charges, delivery timelines, or return-shipping costs apply.

4. No auto-renewal — how billing works

Drut Pro does NOT auto-renew. We do not set up a recurring e-mandate, we do not store any standing instruction to debit you again, and we will not charge you automatically at the end of your billing period. Each Drut Pro purchase is a one-time payment for a single billing term.

When your billing term ends, your Drut Pro access simply lapses and you return to the free tier. To continue with Pro, you manually re-subscribe and make a fresh payment for the next term. You will never be charged again unless you take a clear, affirmative action to pay.

If automatic renewal is introduced in future, it will follow RBI e-mandate rules and you will be notified in advance — including the renewal amount, frequency, and how to cancel — and you will be given an explicit opportunity to opt in before any recurring charge is set up.

5. How to cancel

Because Drut Pro does not auto-renew, there is no recurring charge to switch off — your subscription naturally ends at the close of the term you paid for. You do not need to do anything to stop being billed again.

If you wish to stop using Drut Pro before your term ends, or do not want to continue, you can:

  • Simply not re-subscribe — your access ends automatically at the end of the current term and you move to the free tier; or
  • Stop using Pro at any time — there is no lock-in, and no cancellation charge of any kind. (Under the E-Commerce Rules, we do not impose a cancellation charge on you that we would not also bear ourselves.); or
  • Ask us to close your subscription or account by emailing support@drut.club or calling +91 91001 17027. We will action it and confirm.

Cancelling is at least as easy as subscribing. We will never hide the option to stop, bury it behind confusing steps, or pressure you into staying.

6. What happens to your access after cancellation

If you choose to stop or cancel before your billing term ends, your Drut Pro features remain active until the end of the term you have already paid for. You keep the benefit of what you paid for; you are not cut off early.

At the end of the term (whether you cancelled earlier or simply let it lapse), your account reverts to the free tier, with access to 20 free questions per day. Your account, practice history, scores, and streaks remain intact unless you separately ask to delete your account or data (see our Privacy Policy for account and data deletion).

7. Refund eligibility

Subscription fees are non-refundable once a billing period has started, because you receive immediate, full access to the paid digital service the moment your payment succeeds. This is the default rule.

Business decision — confirm The "non-refundable once the period has started" stance is a business decision for you to confirm. It is permitted under Indian law only if it is conspicuously disclosed before purchase and cancellation is genuinely frictionless (to avoid the prohibited "subscription trap" dark pattern), and it can never override the statutory exceptions below.

This non-refundable rule is subject to the following exceptions, where a refund will be considered or made:

  • (a) Grave Drut-side technical or service failure. If a serious technical or service failure on Drut's side prevents you from using the paid service for a meaningful period, we will assess your case on its individual facts and may issue a full or partial refund. This is assessed case-by-case at Drut's reasonable discretion.
  • (b) Where the law requires a refund. Nothing in this Policy limits your statutory rights. Under the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020 and the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, where a service is defective, deficient, spurious, or not as described or advertised, we will not refuse a refund, and we will refund you as required by applicable law.

In short: the current period is non-refundable by default, except where a grave Drut-side failure prevents you from using the service, or where the law otherwise requires a refund. To request a refund under an exception, contact us using the details in Section 12 and describe the issue.

8. Failed, duplicate & unrecognised payments

All payments are processed by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor. Drut does not collect, store, or have access to your card, UPI, or bank-account details — these are handled entirely by Razorpay in line with RBI's card-data-storage rules.

Failed payments

If a payment fails, your bank or payment provider may briefly hold or show a pending amount that is typically released automatically. No Drut Pro access is granted for a failed payment, and you are free to try again. If money was debited but your account did not upgrade, contact us — see below.

Duplicate or double charges

If you are charged more than once for the same subscription — for example, due to a network or processing error — the duplicate charge is fully refundable. Contact us at support@drut.club with the date, amount, and any payment reference, and we will verify with Razorpay and refund the duplicate to your original payment method.

Money debited but access not granted

If your payment was debited but Drut Pro was not activated, contact us. We will verify the transaction status with Razorpay; if the payment succeeded but access was not delivered, we will either activate your Pro access or, if you prefer, refund the amount in full.

Unrecognised charges

If you see a Drut-related charge you do not recognise, contact us promptly. We will investigate with Razorpay and, where the charge is found to be incorrect or unauthorised on Drut's side, refund it.

9. How refunds are processed & timelines

Where a refund is approved under this Policy, it is processed as follows:

  • Method: refunds are made to your original payment method (the same card, UPI, or bank account used to pay), via Razorpay. We do not refund to a different account or by cash.
  • Initiation: once a refund is approved, we initiate it with Razorpay promptly.
  • Timeline: after we initiate the refund, it is typically credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. The exact time depends on Razorpay and your bank or card issuer, and may occasionally take longer for reasons outside our control.
  • Confirmation: we will confirm by email once the refund has been initiated.

If a refund has not reached you within the stated window, contact us with your details and we will follow up with Razorpay on your behalf.

10. Your consumer rights

Nothing in this Policy takes away your rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019. As a consumer, you have the following six statutory rights, and we are committed to honouring each of them:

  • Right to safety — to be protected against goods and services that are hazardous to life and property.
  • Right to information — to be informed about the quality, quantity, price, and terms of what you are buying, including this refund and cancellation policy, before you pay.
  • Right to choose — to access services at competitive prices, without being forced or pressured, including a genuinely usable free tier.
  • Right to be heard — to have your concerns and complaints listened to and given due consideration.
  • Right to redressal — to seek resolution against unfair trade practices or deficient service, including refunds where the law requires.
  • Right to consumer awareness / education — to acquire the knowledge and skills needed to be an informed consumer.

If you are not satisfied with how we handle a complaint, you may also approach the National Consumer Helpline (call 1915 or visit consumerhelpline.gov.in) and the appropriate Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission.

11. Fair dealing — no dark patterns

We design our pricing, checkout, and cancellation flows to be honest and fair, in line with the CCPA Guidelines for Prevention and Regulation of Dark Patterns, 2023. Specifically, we commit that:

  • The full price, inclusive of applicable taxes, is shown up front — no drip pricing and no surprise charges at the final step.
  • There are no pre-ticked boxes, no pre-selected add-ons, and no sneaked-in donations or charges.
  • Our free tier (20 questions/day) is genuinely usable and does not require card or payment details.
  • We do not use false urgency, fake scarcity, or fake popularity. Any introductory price (such as the ₹1799 first-year offer) has a real basis and stated eligibility.
  • Cancelling — or choosing not to continue — is at least as easy as subscribing, and is never hidden behind confusing steps.
