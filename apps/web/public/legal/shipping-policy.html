1. Drut is a digital service — nothing is shipped

Drut is an online exam-preparation service delivered entirely over the internet through our website (drut.club) and our iOS and Android apps. We do not sell, ship, or deliver any physical goods. There are no couriers, shipping charges, tracking numbers, or delivery addresses involved in any purchase.

2. How you receive what you pay for

When you purchase a Drut Pro subscription, access is delivered electronically and immediately to your Drut account once payment is confirmed by Razorpay. Your Pro features unlock automatically across the web app and any mobile app signed in to the same account — there is nothing to wait for, and nothing arrives by post.

3. Confirming your delivery

You can confirm delivery at any time by opening your Drut profile: an active subscription shows your plan and its validity. You will also receive a payment confirmation from Razorpay for your records.

4. If your access is not delivered

If a payment succeeds but your Pro access does not unlock within a few minutes, please write to support@drut.club or call +91 91001 17027 with your registered email and the payment reference. We will restore access promptly or, where appropriate, address it under our Refund & Cancellation Policy.
